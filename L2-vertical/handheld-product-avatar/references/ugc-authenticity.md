# UGC authenticity (handheld)

## Defaults for prompts

- Vertical 9:16, slight handheld micro-shake (not drunk cam)
- Natural window / practical light; avoid studio softbox look
- Home desk, kitchen counter, balcony, casual street — not seamless gray void
- First-person / selfie angle OR friend-filming mid-shot
- Phone-shot aesthetic OK; shallow depth; imperfect but readable product logo

## Audio tone (for TTS direction)

| Beat | Tone |
|------|------|
| Hook | Higher energy, short punch |
| Problem | Empathy, slightly slower |
| Solution | Confident, clear |
| CTA | Urgency without yelling |

## Avoid

- Perfect studio ring-light beauty ads (unless user asks branded)
- Locked tripod + teleprompter stiffness
- Green-screen / transparent avatar (Phase 2 — not this MVP)
- Wall of text VO; keep spoken length in budget
